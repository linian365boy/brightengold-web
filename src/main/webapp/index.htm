Company Profile

You are here: Home >> ABOUT US >> Company Profile

Shenzhen Hien technology co.,ltd was founded in 2009,formerly Shenzhen Huaen technology co.,ltd,with the devolopment needs,officially changed the company name as Shenzhen Hien technology co.,ltd in 2011.
Hien technology have a own independent R&D team,specialized in all kinds of high-tech eletrical products’ design,production and sale since establishment.Hien’s main product is consumer electronics products,including all kinds of power swicth,adapter,travel charger,power bank,USB wall socket,wireless charger,car jump starters and all kinds of Power Semiconductor Devices.Most of Hien’s product approved the CE,RoHS,FCC certificates,some USB wall socket also approved BS,TUV,ETL certifications.Hien product selling well to worldwide including Europe,America,Africa and Asia.
Hien has it’s own independent brand,is one of the leading power supplier in China.

Hien always regard the " people- oriented, technological innovation " as the company conception since establishment, focus on providing more safety,more environmental and more convenient product to customer.At the same time,Hien’s R&D team specialized in more advanced products’ design and improvement,make sure customers get more excellent product and satisfied service.

Hien will moving forward all the time!
